Rooibos Mocktail

Prep Time: 30 mins

Yeild: 4

Ingredients







Steps

  1. Make a pot of Rooibos tea and add 15 tea bags.
  2. Add 250ml sugar (or sweetener) to the hot Rooibos and let it cool.
  3. Add cold water, pineapple and apricot juice, as well as the lemon juice and bitters to taste.
  4. Enjoy chilled!
