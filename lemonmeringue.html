Lemon Meringue Maltshakes

Prep Time: 30 mins

Yeild: 4

Ingredients










Steps

  1. Combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, condensed milk, lemon zest, lemon juice and Horlicks powder in a high speed blender, and blitz until smooth.
  2. Drizzle the insides of 4 serving glasses with the lemon curd. Pour in the maltshake mixture. Decorate the tops with crushed store-bought mini coconut biscuits, crushed ready-made meringue buttons, store-bought chocolate malt balls and fresh mint.
