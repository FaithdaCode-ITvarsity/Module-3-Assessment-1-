Strawberry and Salted nuts ice cream

Prep Time: 40 mins

Yeild: 5 1/2 dozen

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Use a free-standing mixer or an electric handheld beater to whisk the double-thick/thick cream and icing sugar together until thick and smooth. Fold in the freshly whipped cream, salted nuts, vanilla seeds, salt and mashed strawberries.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a freezer-proof bowl and freeze until firm, about 6 hours. Remove from freezer about 10 minutes before serving.
  3. Enjoy scoops of the ice cream garnished with the fresh strawberries and mint leaves.
