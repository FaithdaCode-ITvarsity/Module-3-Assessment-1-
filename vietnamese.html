Vietnamese Egg Coffee

Prep Time: 15 mins

Yeild: 4

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Add the condensed milk, egg yolks and vanilla essence to a large bowl and beat with an electric mixer until pale and fluffy.
  2. Prepare your espresso/coffee and pour into heatproof serving glasses. Top each glass with the egg yolk mixture. Serve immediately, with spoons for stirring and scooping.
