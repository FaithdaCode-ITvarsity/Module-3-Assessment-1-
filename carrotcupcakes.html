Carrot cupcakes with granadilla white chocolate and cream icing

Prep Time: 40 mins

Yeild: 6

Ingredients




Steps

  1. Melt 100g finely chopped white chocolate in a heatproof bowl in the microwave on high until smooth, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir every 20 seconds. Using an electric mixer, cream 55g softened salted butter, 85g icing sugar and 5ml (1 tsp) vanilla essence together, 1 minute.
  4. Whisk in the melted chocolate until combined.
  5. Add 1 tub (230g) plain cream cheese at room temperature and 75g granadilla pulp, and whisk until smooth.
  6. Pipe the icing onto 12 store-bought carrot muffins and decorate with Easter eggs.
