Chicken caesar salad pasta bowl

Prep Time: 30 mins

Yeild: 4

Ingredients









To Serve: Rotisserie chicken, breast chopped, Penne pasta, cooked Cherry tomatoes, chopped Lettuce, chopped Store Bought Croutons Parmesan cheese block for shavings as garnish.

Steps

  1. ADD egg yolks, garlic, anchovies and 1 ½ tbsp of lemon juice to a blender and blend on high for 15-20seconds
  2. WITH the blender on high speed, gradually pour in the oil, until dressing becomes thick.
  3. ADD in the remaining lemon juice and parmesan cheese. Season to taste to Serve
  4. LAYER the ingredients together in a jar
  5. WHEN READY, tip the salad into a bowl , garnish with parmesan shavings
